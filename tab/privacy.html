Privacy

This extension does NOT collect your grades or any other academic data from you. It only stores the grades as well as other related information (regulation, semester and the subject code) in the extension's local storage. The data is stored locally on your computer and is not sent to any server.

The extension however uses geolocation tracking to record page visits when you open the CGPA calculations page to view how we calculate your CGPA. But don't worry, no personal information about your grades or academic records are collected. The data collected through the geolocation API is used to estimate the number of users as well as their general location. It is to be mentioned that this extension is open source, and the code is available on GitHub for anybody to view. I believe in transparency, and am always happy to receive contributions from the community. If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to me. Thanks for using the extension!
